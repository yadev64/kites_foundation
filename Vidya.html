OUR PROJECTS

Sumedha

Aspire! Dream! Achieve! We are providing free coaching for Civil Service and other competitive exams to the deserving students from rural areas of the country through SUMEDHA CIVIL SERVICE ACADEMY. To strengthen their competitive skills, we offer exclusive mock tests, comprehensive Library facility and orientation seminars.

Aspire

A career guidance and mentorship platform by SUMEDHA that aims to steer the children into right career paths by taking into consideration their interests, talents and core skills. The programme also gives an overview regarding the admissions to various central and foreign universities and how to approch the application processes.

KITES Model School

Elementary education is the foundation of a child's education where a student can be moulded the right way. Kites Foundation aims to make schools child friendly by providing best infrastructure and creating an environment where a child can enjoy learning process, thereby bringing out the best the student.

E-Shiksha

Georgraphical boundaries should never be a hindrance to your learning process. Come join our digital literacy program powered by state of art e- learning.
