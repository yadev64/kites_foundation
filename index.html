Sumedha

Sumedha is the higher education programme aims at training students from primary to post graduate levels to tackle competitive exams by providing them with necessary trainings to scale heights. Sumedha also helps to discover the creative skills in students through various programmes undertaken by the initiative thus moulding individuals who are capable of nation building.

Punarjjani

Punarjjani is an agri project in lines of state government's Subiksha Keralam Programme. As part of the project, Kites Foundation is undertaking 1000 acres of fallow land accross the state. Apart from 1000 acres of tillage programme the foundation is also undertaking several other programmes including agricultural seminars, a portal for supporting farming initiatives, different cultural competitions etc.

Our PROJECTS

out of the BOX

A creativity enhancement workshop to bring happiness and perspective development in children through film making as a tool.The campaign helps in exploring the limitless potential within each child to be the best versions of themselves.

Summer Pots

The Summer Pots campaign is a Kites Initiative to protect animals from summer strokes and in order to promote the idea of co-existence. The campaign witnessed a participation of more than a thousand kind-hearted people all over the world.

Quizona

Kites in association with Prajna Quiz Club, conducted an online daily quiz program 'QUIZONA' in order to engage people with knowledge sharing during the times of lockdown and quarantine. Many young minds won exciting prizes!
